Our Vision

Despite encountering challenges, our dedication and commitment to this initiative has led to the successful and inclusive organisation of gatherings that have brought together individuals from diverse backgrounds over the past three years. We aspire to continue this trajectory into the future.

To ensure the sustainability of these events, enhance our impact, and uphold our initiatives, we kindly request your support in the following areas:

  • Securing a permanent venue for our community events to guarantee their continuity.
  • Providing guidance and assistance in orchestrating larger-scale events, especially for the festive celebrations of Diwali and Holi.
  • Extending financial support to sustain these events in the long run for the advancement of our community.
