About Us

Bringing Community, Culture, and Spirituality Together

Our Story

ADCS was established by a group of Ashford residents 2021 who identified a gap in social engagement within our community. Recognising the importance of providing opportunities for our youth to showcase their talents and creating a secure environment for the elderly to engage in cultural events and activities, we embarked on this initiative.

Our Mission

Our aim was to foster inclusivity and unity within the local community by sharing our rich cultural diversity and nurturing a strong communal harmony.

Our Vision

This endeavor was driven by the belief that uniting our community and embracing positivity and festive spirit throughout the year was essential
